Meet the Team

Welcome to our spotlight — a cheerful group of teens who run community projects and weekend meetups. Below each member has a photo and a short bio.

Lead

Sam Rivera

Sam organizes our weekend coding workshops and loves robotics. They are known for attention to detail and bringing snacks.

Interests: coding, robotics, skateboarding.

Coordinator

Aisha Patel

Aisha coordinates outreach and social media for the group. She designs flyers and makes sure our events are welcoming to newcomers.

Interests: graphic design, photography, volunteer work.

Treasurer

Jordan Lee

Jordan manages the group's budget and logistics. They also run a small game-dev club where members learn to build games together.

Interests: game development, math puzzles, hiking.

Outreach

Riley Gomez

Riley focuses on community partnerships and organizes field trips. They bring energy to meetings and keep everyone connected.

Interests: community engagement, music, biking.

Volunteer

Terry Nguyen

Terry volunteers at events, helps set up equipment, and mentors new members in introductory workshops.

Interests: mentoring, coding, board games.
